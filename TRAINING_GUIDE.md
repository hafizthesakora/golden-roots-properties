## 1. Getting Started — Logging In

### How to access the platform
1. Open your browser (Chrome or Safari recommended)
2. Go to the app URL provided by your manager
3. You will see a **Sign In** screen

### Signing in
- Enter your **email address** and **password**
- Click **Sign In**
- If it's your first time, click **Sign Up** to create an account with your name, email, and a password

### What you see after logging in
You will land on the **Dashboard** — your main home screen showing a summary of everything happening in the workspace.

## 2. Installing the App on Your Phone

The platform can be installed on your phone just like a regular app, so you can open it from your home screen without opening a browser.

### On Android phones (Chrome browser)
1. A banner will appear at the bottom of the screen saying **"Install Golden Roots"**
2. Tap the **Install App** button
3. Confirm — the app icon will appear on your home screen
4. You're done! Open it like any other app

### On iPhone (Safari browser)
> **Important:** You must use Safari on iPhone, not Chrome.

1. A banner will appear at the bottom — tap **Install App**
2. A guide will pop up showing 3 steps:
   - **Step 1:** Tap the **Share button** (the box with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom of Safari)
   - **Step 2:** Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
   - **Step 3:** Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
3. The Golden Roots icon will appear on your home screen

### If you don't see the banner
- Make sure you are using the correct browser (Chrome on Android, Safari on iPhone)
- The banner only shows on a secure (HTTPS) connection
- Try refreshing the page

## 3. The Dashboard — Your Home Screen

The Dashboard gives you a quick overview of everything happening in the workspace at a glance.

### What you'll see

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Total Tasks** | How many tasks exist this month |
| **Completed** | How many tasks are done (with % completion) |
| **Overdue** | Tasks that are past their due date — needs attention |
| **Active Leads** | Number of leads currently being worked on |
| **Tasks by Status** | A pie chart showing task breakdown |
| **Lead Pipeline** | Progress bars showing leads at each stage |
| **Recent Tasks** | The 5 most recently created tasks |
| **Projects** | Quick list of your workspace projects |
| **Team** | Members in the workspace |
| **Recent Activity** | A live log of everything that's happened recently |

### Tips
- Numbers count up when the page loads — this is normal
- The dashboard refreshes automatically every 12 seconds — you don't need to reload
- Red numbers in "Overdue" need immediate attention
- Click **View All** on any section to see the full list

## 4. Leads Pipeline — Managing Property Leads

This is where you track every potential client from the first contact all the way to closing a deal.

### Understanding the Lead Stages

Leads move from left to right as they progress:

```
NEW → CONTACTED → QUALIFIED → PROPOSAL → NEGOTIATION → CLOSED WON ✓
                                                      → CLOSED LOST ✗
```

| Stage | What it means |
|---|---|
| **New** | Just added, no contact made yet |
| **Contacted** | You have reached out to them |
| **Qualified** | They are a genuine buyer/renter with budget |
| **Proposal** | You have sent them a property proposal |
| **Negotiation** | Discussing price and terms |
| **Closed Won** | Deal successful! |
| **Closed Lost** | They did not proceed |

### How to add a new lead

1. Click the **Add Lead** button (amber/gold colour)
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Full Name** — required
   - **Phone** — optional (format: +63 9xx xxx xxxx)
   - **Email** — optional
   - **Lead Source** — where did this lead come from?
     - Social Media, Referral, Website, Walk-in, Cold Call, Event, Other
   - **Stage** — what stage are they at right now?
   - **Property Type** — Residential, Commercial, Land, or Rental
   - **Budget** — their budget range (e.g. ₱3M – ₱5M)
   - **Property of Interest** — specific property they asked about
   - **Notes** — anything else worth noting
3. Click **Add Lead**

### How to update a lead

1. Click on any lead card on the board
2. The edit form opens — two tabs at the top:
   - **Details** — update any of their information
   - **Notes & History** — see all notes and status changes with timestamps
3. To move them to a new stage, change the **Stage** dropdown
4. Click **Save Changes**

### Moving leads on the board
- You can drag and drop lead cards from one column to another
- The stage updates automatically when you drop it

### Deleting a lead
- Open the lead → click the red **Delete** button
- Confirm in the popup — **this cannot be undone**

## 5. Tasks — Managing Your Work

Tasks are the day-to-day work items assigned to team members. You can view them in three ways.

### Three ways to view tasks

| View | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Table** | Seeing all tasks in a list with details |
| **Kanban** | Visualising work in progress (like the leads board) |
| **Calendar** | Seeing tasks by their due date |

Switch between views using the tabs at the top of the Tasks page.

### Task statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Backlog** | Not started yet, low priority |
| **To Do** | Ready to work on |
| **In Progress** | Currently being worked on |
| **In Review** | Done, waiting for approval/check |
| **Done** | Completed |

### My Tasks vs All Tasks
- **My Tasks** (sidebar) — only shows tasks assigned to YOU
- **All Tasks** (sidebar) — shows every task across the whole workspace

### How to create a task

1. Click the **+ New Task** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Task Name** — what needs to be done (required)
   - **Due Date** — when it needs to be done by
   - **Assignee** — who is responsible for it
   - **Status** — starting status (usually "To Do")
   - **Project** — which project it belongs to
   - **Recurring Task** — toggle ON if this repeats regularly
     - Choose how often: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly
     - When you mark it Done, the next one is created automatically
3. Click **Create Task**

### How to update a task
1. Click any task to open it
2. Change any fields — status, assignee, due date
3. Changes save automatically
4. **Comments** — scroll down to add notes or updates that the whole team can see

### Filtering tasks
Use the filter bar to narrow down tasks by:
- **Status** (e.g. show only In Progress)
- **Assignee** (e.g. show only your tasks)
- **Project** (e.g. show only tasks for a specific project)
- **Due Date**

## 6. Content Calendar — Planning Posts

This is where you plan all your social media and marketing content. Every post you plan shows up on a calendar so you can see the whole month at a glance.

### How to add a content post

1. Click **+ Add Content**
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Title** — name of the post (required)
   - **Concept / Description** — what the post is about (required)
   - **Post Type** — what kind of content?
     - Flyer, Video, Reel, Story, Carousel, Graphic, Other
   - **Platform** — where will it be posted?
     - Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, or All Platforms
   - **Status** — where is it in the process?
     - Draft → Scheduled → Published → Cancelled
   - **Scheduled Date** — which day should it go out? (required)
   - **Program / Festivity** — is it tied to an event? (e.g. Christmas, Open House)
   - **Tags** — keywords for organisation (e.g. promo, listing)
3. Click **Save Content**

### Navigating the calendar
- Use the **←** and **→** arrows to go to previous/next month
- Posts appear as coloured cards on their scheduled date
- Click any post to edit or update its status

### Content status workflow
```
Draft → Scheduled → Published
              ↓
          Cancelled
```

## 7. File Repository — Team Documents

This is the central place to store and share all team documents — contracts, property images, brochures, agreements, and more.

### How to upload a file

1. Click **Upload File**
2. Fill in the form:
   - **File** — click to select a file from your device (max size: 50MB)
   - **File Name** — it fills in automatically, but you can rename it
   - **Category** — what type of file is it?
     - Document, Contract, Image, Video, Spreadsheet, Presentation, Invoice, Other
   - **Description** — optional notes about the file
3. Click **Upload to Drive**

### Finding files
- Use the **search bar** to search by file name
- Use the **Category** filter to show only one type of file
- Files are sorted newest first

### Downloading a file
- Find the file in the list
- Click the **Download** button on the file card

### Deleting a file
- Click the **Delete** button on the file card
- Confirm — **this cannot be undone**

### Managing categories
- Click the **Categories** button to add your own custom categories
- Default categories cannot be deleted

## 8. Members — Your Team

This page shows everyone who has access to the workspace.

### Member roles

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage members, settings, and all content |
| **Member** | Can view and edit tasks, leads, content, and files |

### How to invite someone to the workspace

1. Go to **Settings** (bottom of the left sidebar)
2. Find the **Invite Members** section
3. Click **Copy Invite Link**
4. Send that link to the person you want to invite (via WhatsApp, email, etc.)
5. They open the link, sign up or log in, and they're added automatically

### Changing someone's role
- Go to **Members** in the sidebar
- Find the person and click the role dropdown next to their name
- Change to **Admin** or **Member**
- Only Admins can do this

### Removing a member
- Go to **Members**
- Click the **Remove** button next to their name
- Confirm — they will lose access immediately

## 9. Settings — Workspace Preferences

Access Settings from the bottom of the left sidebar.

### What you can change
- **Workspace Name** — rename the workspace
- **Workspace Icon** — upload a logo or image (JPG, PNG, max 1MB)

### Invite Link
- **Copy Invite Link** — share this link to invite new members
- **Reset Invite Link** — creates a new link (the old link stops working)

### Danger Zone
- **Delete Workspace** — permanently deletes everything
  - ⚠️ This cannot be undone. Only do this if you are absolutely sure.
